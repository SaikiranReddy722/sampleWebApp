Hello Milky Way

The conventional method of driving relies on visual input to make decisions. Our constant reference point for lane orientation on the road is provided by the lines marking our lanes. One of the fundamental and initial tasks in the development of a self-driving car is the implementation of an algorithm for the automatic detection of lane lines. The primary objective of Lane Detection and Assistance Systems is to provide accurate real-time information about the vehicle's position within the lane and to assist the driver or autonomous vehicle in maintaining safe and precise lane-keeping.
